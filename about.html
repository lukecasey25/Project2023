When We Began

Back in 1995, Luke Greg Casey and his stepson Phil Dunphy began taking on small jobs around their town to make extra money. Upon an unprecidented rise in demand, Luke and Phil decided to quit their midweek jobs and do Landscaping full-time.

From Start-up to Scale-up

After Three years, Luke and Greg decided that they needed more help and to broaden their horizons. This resulted in taking jobs outside of dublin and hiring additional employees. The business grew from strength to strength.
